This document describes the commercial licensing path for organizations that want to use codexSync code outside the rights granted by GPL-3.0-or-later.

## When a Commercial License Is Needed

A commercial license is required if you want to do any of the following with codexSync or derivative works without complying with GPL-3.0-or-later obligations:

- Distribute a closed-source product that includes codexSync code.
- Embed codexSync code in paid software offerings without releasing full corresponding source under GPL terms.
- Offer contractual terms to your customers that conflict with GPL-3.0-or-later.

## Internal Use

If your organization uses codexSync only internally (for your own employees/contractors) and does not distribute the software outside your organization, you may use it under GPL-3.0-or-later terms.

## Commercial Terms

Commercial use rights are available under a separate paid agreement from the copyright holder(s).

To request terms, contact the project owner and include:

- Company name
- Intended use case
- Distribution model
- Expected number of users/installations
